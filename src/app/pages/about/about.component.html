Who am I?


Your hands, at a glance.


Hello! My name is Milyuris, and I'm thankful to be fulfilling my dream of becoming a proud nail technician. Alana Nails Studio started with one mission: help people find innovation in the display of their hands. Whether with acrylics, gels or a simple manicure, I'm pleased to serve your nails.


Always eager to learn.


As a dedicated nail technician, I am proud to hold certifications from the American Association of Nail Technicians (AANT) and the International Nail Technicians Association (INTA). These certifications validate my proficiency in a wide range of nail care techniques including acrylics, gels, and nail artistry. My commitment to staying updated with the latest trends enables me to offer my clients innovative and personalized nail care solutions.


Honoring a joyful companion.


Alana Nails Studio is named after our playful dog, Alana the lab. Seeing her tail wagging and hearing her barking would always put a smile in our face. Her friendliness and welcoming attributes capture the essence of this studio and make up its foundations. After all, a smile is all it takes to change someone's day.
